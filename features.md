# Features

## Spells

The spell wheel is acessed with 'X' by default. You can quick cast the last used spell with 'V'.

## Cryptic eye

Spawn with you in the world, can be feed totem of undying to boost your max health, when it has eaten enough totem, it can act as one

## Lucky Horseshoe

Findable in chests, chance to negate fall damage, upgradeable to increase chance.

## Eternal Guardian

A ring spawn in the ancient city, you need to suck the souls of mobs to attune to it.  
When you die with it equipped and attuned, a tombstone is created with all your items and xp and a mob spawn guarding it. Other player can loot your tombstone if they kill the guardian first.  
When you kill a guardian, you drop it's head, you can make a mask out of it which give you a spell you can use to apply the glowing effect to surrounding entity. The more you wear the mask the more powerfull the spell become.

## Cryptic Codex

In-game documentation, accessed with 'H' by default.
